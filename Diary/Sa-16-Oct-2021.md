# Writing of day
Peeling skins off my fingers, the callus formed from climbing once a week now. Make me think how hard it is to maintain #habit, even when it felt really good when we started it. The season changed, our schedule changed and the initial motivation died down. Our focus moved to something else. Things that rightfully should take our focus and energy. But the original habit is important and something needs to be nurtured. Most of the hard part is just starting it. Start the 5 minute work of it. Once our life is filled with the things we want to foster and nurture, the bad habit will NOT have enough room in our life and will slowly get pushed away. I think this was part of the reason I liked going to work a lot. The setting and environment forced me to focus on a few small things. It brought structure to my life. The act of leaving the laptop at office and never taking it home was glories. Only checking emails after I have had my morning office and commuted to the office. That context switch meant a lot, the lack of intermediate space/time between context switch make the transition a lot harder.
