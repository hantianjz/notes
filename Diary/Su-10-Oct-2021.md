# Notes
- #MECE describe a way of organizing information. The MECE principle suggests that to understand and fix any large problem, you need to understand your options by sorting them into categories that are: 
    - **Mutually Exclusive** : Items can only fit into one category at a time 
    - **Collectively Exhaustive** : All items can fit into one of the categories.
	
- #Pyramid_Principle  The key take-aways from the Pyramid Principle at McKinsey were:
    1.  Start with the answer first.
    2.  Group and summarize your supporting arguments.
    3.  Logically order your supporting ideas.
